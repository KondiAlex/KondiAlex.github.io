About

I hold a PhD in Physics and work on computational nanometrology, AI for science, and machine learning for the characterization of micro- and nanostructures. My research focuses on SEM-based metrology, image analysis, morphology quantification, and data-driven modeling for semiconductor manufacturing and advanced materials.

Research Interests

  • Computational nanometrology
  • SEM-based metrology & image analysis
  • Machine learning for materials characterization
  • Morphology quantification of micro-/nanostructures
  • AI for science & semiconductor manufacturing
